Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers that store your personal information. Your meals, weight, exercise logs, and goals never leave your iPhone.

Do I need to create an account?

No. CaloriePro works immediately without any account or sign-up. Just download, complete the quick setup, and start tracking.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will delete your data. We recommend using the Export feature (Profile > Export Data) to back up your data regularly. You can export as JSON and re-import it later.

How accurate are the calorie counts?

Our food database contains nutritional data for over 2,000 common foods. Barcode scans use the Open Food Facts database. While we strive for accuracy, all calorie and nutrition values are estimates. For precise dietary requirements, please consult a registered dietitian.

How does the barcode scanner work?

Point your camera at a product barcode. The app reads the barcode and looks up nutrition information from Open Food Facts, a free, collaborative food database. No personal data is sent ‚Äî only the barcode number.

Can I export my data?

Yes! CaloriePro offers multiple export options: Excel reports (.xlsx) with 5 formatted worksheets, visual HTML reports you can share or print, CSV files for use in spreadsheets, and JSON backup of your complete data.

How are exercise calories calculated?

We use MET values (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) ‚Äî the gold standard for estimating energy expenditure. The formula is: Calories = MET √ó weight(kg) √ó duration(hours). Your weight from your profile is used for personalised calculations.

Does CaloriePro work offline?

Yes. All core features work without an internet connection, including food logging, exercise tracking, and viewing reports. The only feature that requires internet is barcode scanning (to look up product data).

Can I change between dark and light mode?

Yes. Tap your profile icon, then tap the theme toggle (sun/moon icon) to switch between dark and light themes. The app also follows your device's system theme by default.

üîß Troubleshooting

Barcode scanner not working

Ensure you've granted camera permission. Go to iPhone Settings > CaloriePro > Camera and make sure it's enabled. Try holding the barcode steady in good lighting.

Data not saving

CaloriePro uses your device's local storage. If your device storage is nearly full, the app may not be able to save data. Free up some space and try again.

App feels slow

If you have a very large amount of logged data (thousands of entries), the app may take a moment to load. Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, export your data and use the Reset option in Profile to start fresh, then import your backup.
