CaloriePro is a calorie, macro, and exercise tracker designed for UK users. It includes a database of 5,000+ UK-specific foods and 400+ exercises. The app works offline and stores all data locally on your device ‚Äî no account needed, no data collected.

Privacy & Data
Where is my data stored? ‚ñº

Everything is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. Your meals, weight, exercises, and settings never leave your phone or computer. We have no servers that store user data.

Can you see my data? ‚ñº

No. We literally cannot see your data. There are no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no server-side storage. Your health information stays on your device and is only accessible to you.

What happens if I clear my browser data? ‚ñº

Clearing browser data or uninstalling the app will delete all your CaloriePro data. We strongly recommend using the Backup feature regularly ‚Äî go to the Data & Support section to export a backup file you can restore later.

Can I export my data? ‚ñº

Yes! You can export your data in multiple formats ‚Äî JSON (for backup/restore), CSV (for spreadsheets), Excel, and PDF reports. Go to the Reports or Data & Support section in the app.

Features
How accurate is the food database? ‚ñº

Our database contains 5,000+ UK foods with calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat values. Values are sourced from manufacturer data and nutritional databases. As with any tracker, values are estimates ‚Äî actual nutrition can vary between batches and preparation methods.

Can I add my own foods? ‚ñº

Yes. You can add custom foods with your own calorie and macro values. Custom foods are saved to your device and appear alongside the built-in database when searching.

Does it work offline? ‚ñº

Yes, CaloriePro works fully offline once loaded. It's a Progressive Web App (PWA) that caches everything on your device. You can track meals, log exercises, and view reports without any internet connection.

What exercise types are supported? ‚ñº

CaloriePro supports 400+ exercises across cardio and strength training. Cardio exercises calculate calories based on duration and MET values. Strength exercises track sets, reps, and weight with accurate calorie calculations.

Can I track past days? ‚ñº

Yes. Use the date navigation on the dashboard to go back to any previous day. You can add, edit, or delete meals and exercises for past dates.

2. Close and reopen the app.

3. If installed as a PWA, uninstall and reinstall it.

If the issue persists, contact us at caloriepro@gmail.com.

I lost all my data ‚ñº

If you have a backup file, go to Data & Support ‚Üí Import/Restore and select your backup. If you don't have a backup, unfortunately the data cannot be recovered ‚Äî this is the trade-off of local-only storage. We recommend exporting a backup regularly.
