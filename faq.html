üì± Using the App

How do I log a meal?

There are several ways:

  • Tap the green + button (always visible at the bottom)
  • Search for foods in the database (5,000+ UK foods)
  • Scan a barcode using the camera
  • Enter food details manually
  • Use Build-a-Meal to combine multiple foods
  • Reuse saved meal templates
Can I log meals for past days?

Yes! Use the arrow buttons on the dashboard to navigate between days, or tap any day on the calendar to view and edit that day's entries.

How does the calorie target work?

CaloriePro calculates your target using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is scientifically validated. It considers your age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and goal (lose/maintain/gain). You can also set custom targets in Profile settings.

What's the mood tracker for?

Research shows diet affects mood. Track how you feel each day to spot patterns ‚Äî you might notice you feel better on days you eat more protein, drink more water, or exercise. It's a simple way to connect your food choices to your wellbeing.

üîí Privacy & Data

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your phone. We have no servers collecting your information ‚Äî your health data is completely private.

Can I backup my data?

Yes! Go to Profile ‚Üí Backup to Files for a quick backup, or use Export Report for PDF, CSV, or JSON formats. You can restore from a JSON backup at any time.

Yes! Export your data as JSON on your old device, transfer the file (via AirDrop, email, iCloud, etc.), then import it on your new device using Profile ‚Üí Restore.

üèÉ Exercise & Nutrition

How are exercise calories calculated?

We use MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values ‚Äî the scientific standard for measuring exercise intensity. Calories burned = MET √ó body weight (kg) √ó duration (hours). Your weight is taken from your profile for accurate calculations.

What macro targets should I set?

By default, CaloriePro uses balanced targets: 25% protein, 50% carbs, 25% fat. For weight loss, some prefer higher protein (30-35%). For athletes, carbs might be higher. You can set custom targets in Profile ‚Üí Custom Macro Targets.

Are the food calories accurate?

Our database uses official UK nutrition data where available. For branded items (Greggs, Nando's, Costa, etc.), we use values from their published nutrition information. The barcode scanner pulls data from the OpenFoodFacts database. You can always adjust values or add custom foods if needed.
