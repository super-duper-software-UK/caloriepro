1

App not loading or looks broken

Force-refresh the page by pulling down on mobile, or pressing Ctrl+Shift+R on desktop. If installed as a PWA, try uninstalling and reinstalling.

2

Lost your data

If you have a backup, go to Data & Support ‚Üí Import/Restore in the app. Without a backup, data cannot be recovered as it's stored locally. We recommend backing up regularly.

3

Food not in the database

You can add custom foods with your own calorie and macro values. Tap the "+" button and choose "Add Custom Food". Your custom entries are saved alongside the built-in database.

4

Export or backup not working

Make sure you're using a supported browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android). Try using the Share option if the direct download doesn't work.
